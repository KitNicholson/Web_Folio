About

I am a graphic design student from the University of Melbourne, and absolutely passionate about good design. I don’t limit my practice to any style or niche, with work ranging from branding to book design, typography, animation, and generative work.

Whichever area I work in my process begins with research on the topic, that is turned into work through play and experimentation. Each project then goes through a period of refinement to create the final work.

My interests lie in creating designs with a strong sense of style. Whether it’s a maximalist poster or simple book cover, creating visually unique work that can communicate the nuances of each project is the goal.
